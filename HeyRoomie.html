Our Approach

Discover Community, Discover
Home.

At HeyRoomie, we believe that a true home is more than just four walls; it's where connections flourish and memories are made. Everyone deserves a space that feels just right, and our smart, data-driven algorithm is here to make that happen. By matching you with great vibes and like-minded roommates, we're not just crafting homes—we're building a community where friendships thrive and every moment feels at home.

Our Values

Our Values

Shared living is at the heart of everything we do. And not just with any roommate—it's about building a home together. We can't create the perfect living space alone, so we make great partnerships the foundation of our community. We believe these three core values are what it takes to foster those great partnerships.

Authenticity

We share — never hide — our words, actions, and intentions.

Synergy

Together, we create something greater than the sum of our individual parts, fostering collaboration that enhances our shared living experience.

Community

We build meaningful connections that transform shared spaces into true homes where everyone belongs.

Our Principles

We bring our values to life. Our principles guide us to prioritize our values in every decision we make.


01

Embrace the Challenge

True innovation emerges from a deep understanding of the challenges we face, and each setback thrills us to work harder rather than rushing to find quick solutions.

02

Simplify Solutions

We focus on creating straightforward solutions that align with our key objectives—both as an organization and within our teams. We choose to say "no" to many ideas in order to concentrate on what truly matters.

03

Decide with Purpose

Every decision at HeyRoomie is made thoughtfully and intentionally. Our choices are guided by our principles, not by random personal biases.

04

Cultivate Trust

At HeyRoomie, we prioritize trust among each other and actively work to build and restore it through openness and collaboration. When trust is lacking, we address it directly.
